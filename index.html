Your Nature Escape in the Heart of Pampanga

Jungle Base Coffee is a cozy local coffee spot nestled in Telabastagan, Pampanga. We serve premium coffee, delicious pastries, and hearty meals — all while letting you unwind in a calm, nature-inspired setting.

Whether you’re here for your morning cup, brunch with friends, or a quiet spot to work, Jungle Base Coffee brings you closer to nature while enjoying quality food and drinks.
